Hello! My name is Helen Castaneda. I am from Guatemala a beautiful small country in Central America. I am married and have three handsome boys. I decided to go back to school to give my kids a better future. I want to set an example for them to always work hard for their dreams. I want to inspire them to get a better education and become good men.

I enjoy watching tv and spending time with my kids. They are very active and I love to see them play and laugh.
